# Executive Product Mental Models

## Purpose
This repository codifies the mental models, frameworks, and structured thinking systems required for executive-level product leadership.

It is designed to support high-quality decision-making across strategy, prioritization, execution, and organizational leadership.

---

## What This Repo Is

- A structured system of product leadership frameworks
- A decision-support library for real-world product scenarios
- A tool for executive-level interview preparation
- A reference for applying consistent thinking across complex problems

---

## What This Repo Is NOT

- A generic list of business frameworks
- A collection of MBA notes
- An unstructured knowledge dump
- A replacement for hands-on experience

---

## Who This Is For

- Executive product leaders (VP, Head of Product, CPO-track)
- Product operators working across strategy and execution
- Candidates preparing for senior product leadership interviews

---

## How to Use This Repo

- Start with `START_HERE.md`
- Review `REPO_MAP.md` to understand structure
- Navigate to a domain (e.g., strategy, prioritization)
- Apply frameworks to real-world scenarios or interview preparation
- Use examples and templates to operationalize thinking

---

## Structure Overview

This repository is organized into core decision domains:

- Strategy
- Prioritization
- Portfolio Management
- Execution
- Operating Model
- Customer Value
- Risk & Tradeoffs
- Leadership & Communication
- Interview Application

Each framework includes:
- What it is
- When to use it
- When NOT to use it
- Strengths and limitations
- Example application
- Interview framing
