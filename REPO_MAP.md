# Repository Map

This repository is organized around the core decision domains of executive product leadership.

---

## Core Domains

### /strategy
Frameworks for:
- market positioning
- product vision
- strategic direction
- competitive analysis

---

### /prioritization
Frameworks for:
- resource allocation
- roadmap decisions
- tradeoff evaluation
- opportunity sizing

---

### /portfolio-management
Frameworks for:
- investment distribution
- portfolio balancing
- initiative evaluation
- strategic alignment

---

### /execution
Frameworks for:
- delivery management
- operational health
- execution tradeoffs
- delivery confidence

---

### /operating-model
Frameworks for:
- team structure
- process design
- organizational alignment
- scaling product organizations

---

### /customer-value
Frameworks for:
- value identification
- outcome evaluation
- customer impact
- success measurement

---

### /risk
Frameworks for:
- uncertainty management
- tradeoff analysis
- decision risk evaluation

---

### /leadership
Frameworks for:
- communication
- stakeholder alignment
- executive influence
- decision framing

---

### /interview-application
Structured applications of frameworks for:
- answering interview questions
- scenario-based thinking
- executive storytelling

---

## Supporting Assets

### /templates
Reusable formats for:
- applying frameworks
- structuring decisions
- preparing interview answers

---

### /examples
Real or simulated applications of frameworks in practice.

---

## Recommended Navigation Path

If you are new:

1. Start with /strategy
2. Move to /prioritization
3. Explore /execution
4. Apply via /interview-application

---

## Design Principle

This repository is structured to reflect:

**How executive product leaders actually think —  
not how frameworks are typically taught.**
