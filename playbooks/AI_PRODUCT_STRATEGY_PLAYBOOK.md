# AI Product Strategy Playbook

## Objective

Design product strategies that fully leverage AI rather than treating it as an add-on.

## Step 1 — Start with the Problem

- define user pain clearly
- avoid starting with AI capability

## Step 2 — Identify Transformation Points

- where can AI:
  - remove friction
  - automate work
  - unlock new behavior

## Step 3 — Redesign the Workflow

- do not layer AI on top
- redesign the experience end-to-end

## Step 4 — Avoid Common Traps

- no “AI sprinkle” features
- no tech-first solutions
- no copy-paste strategy

## Step 5 — Validate with Users

- measure real usage
- focus on retention, not demos

## Success Criteria

- meaningful user impact
- clear differentiation
- sustained engagement
