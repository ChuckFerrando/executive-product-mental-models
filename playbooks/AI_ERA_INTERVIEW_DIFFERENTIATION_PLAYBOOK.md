# AI Era Interview Differentiation Playbook

## Objective

Stand out in AI-era hiring by demonstrating judgment, perspective, and product-level thinking instead of tool usage.

## Core Principle

> Do not differentiate on AI usage. Differentiate on what you do after AI produces an answer.

## What Not To Do

- Do not focus on tools (ChatGPT, agents, etc.)
- Do not emphasize speed or output volume
- Do not present generic AI fluency

## What To Do Instead

### 1. Demonstrate Judgment

Show how you:
- evaluate AI outputs
- identify risks and edge cases
- make tradeoffs

### 2. Show Product System Thinking

Be able to explain:
- why a model was chosen
- how the system is structured
- what the control layer is
- how it ties to user outcomes

### 3. Bring Perspective

Arrive with:
- a view on the company’s product gaps
- AI opportunities
- potential risks or blind spots

You do not need to be correct. You need to be thoughtful.

### 4. Be Ready for Live Validation

Expect:
- real-time problem solving
- building or thinking exercises
- unscripted evaluation

## Key Reframe

Treat AI like a high-performing but imperfect employee:

- leverage strengths
- anticipate blind spots
- retain final decision ownership

## Success Criteria

- You demonstrate decision quality, not just knowledge
- You show system-level understanding
- You present original thinking, not rehearsed answers
