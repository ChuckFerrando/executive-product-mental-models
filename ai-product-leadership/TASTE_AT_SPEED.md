# Taste at Speed

## Definition

The ability to rapidly evaluate, filter, and select the best options from a large set of AI-generated outputs.

## Core Principle

> When building becomes cheap, selection becomes the bottleneck.

## System Shift

### Before AI
- Building was expensive
- Planning and specs were critical
- Few options were explored

### After AI
- Building is cheap and fast
- Many options can be generated
- Selection becomes the critical skill

## Mechanism

1. Generate multiple versions
2. Evaluate each version
3. Kill low-quality options (~80%)
4. Select highest-potential direction
5. Iterate

## Key Insight

Speed without taste leads to:

> building the wrong things faster

## Components of Taste

- Customer empathy
- Product intuition
- Strategic alignment
- Quality judgment
- Creative vision

## Failure Mode

- Over-indexing on speed
- Shipping low-quality ideas quickly
- Becoming a “feature factory”

## Differentiation

High-performing leaders:
- generate many options
- evaluate rigorously
- make confident selections
- iterate quickly

## Application

Use this model to:

- guide product discovery
- evaluate prototypes
- improve decision-making
- structure AI workflows
